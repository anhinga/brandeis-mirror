Kitchen - temporary closed

What is kitchen, and why one wants to keep it open to everybody? Kitchen is the place where we try to cook our dishes. What are we going to keep here? All kinds of pieces of untested ideas, small problems, unfinished solutions, etc. We will try to keep it sanitary, but there are no guarantees - use it at your own risk (but if you cook something tasty with these ingredients, please acknowledge their use). Why to keep it open? To stimulate thought, to save things which look as they might be good and which would perish otherwise, and because we do not care about so called "priority issues" all that much.
